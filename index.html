💎 What is TMA Five Diamond Certification?

The TMA Five Diamond Certification is the alarm industry's highest recognition for monitoring centers, awarded only to facilities demonstrating exceptional excellence across five critical areas:

  • ⭐Technical Performance: State-of-the-art equipment and redundant systems
  • ⭐Personnel Training: Rigorous ongoing education and certification
  • ⭐Quality Service: Proven track record of accurate emergency response
  • ⭐Customer Satisfaction: Industry-leading service standards
  • ⭐Operational Excellence: Best-in-class procedures and protocols

Less than 5% of monitoring centers nationwide achieve this certification. All ADT monitoring centers maintain Five Diamond status.

🛡️ What is UL Certification?

UL (Underwriters Laboratories) is an independent, globally recognized safety science organization. UL Certification means our facilities meet the most stringent safety standards in the security industry:

  • ✓UL 827: Central station alarm services meet rigorous safety standards
  • ✓UL 1981: Facilities built to withstand natural disasters
  • ✓UL 2050: National industrial security and alarm systems standards
  • ✓Annual Audits: Unannounced inspections ensure continuous compliance

UL Certification guarantees your monitoring center can operate during power outages, natural disasters, and cyber threats.
